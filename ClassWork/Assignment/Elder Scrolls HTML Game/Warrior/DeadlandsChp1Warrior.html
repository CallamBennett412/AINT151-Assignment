The Deadlands: An Elder Scrolls Adventure

Chapter One:

The Flesh Spire

Prison Cage

As your eyes flicker open you notice a sharp pain in your head. As your eyes begin to adjust and unblur you see blood covering the floor, obsidian walls towering over your head, and strange dark magics pulsing through the room. You are trapped, stuck behind blackened bars smeared with dried blood, no visible way to escape.
