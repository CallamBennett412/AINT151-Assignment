Prison Cage

You search the cage for a lock, your finger sliding along the cold ebony metal and crusty blood. After a few minutes of searching you notice that this cage doesn’t have a lock which baffles you. It must be locked via magical means… Suddenly you hear a noise! Someone is walking up the ramp towards you!
