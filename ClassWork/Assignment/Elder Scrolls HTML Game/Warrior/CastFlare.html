Prison Cage

Even though you’re no mage you do know one spell. As you reach out your arm a small ball of flame shoots out towards the Dremora. The Flame ignites the Dremora briefly but you realise that it hasn’t done anything other than annoy him. Look’s like Dremora are immune to fire!
