The Deadlands: An Elder Scrolls Adventure

Tower of Damned Souls

You meet the Dremora head on your sword meeting the side of his armour. However your flare set the Churl into a deadly rage! He ignores any pain as your sword slowly slides into his body, swinging his mace into the side of your head. You fall to the floor letting go of your blade, you barely get to your feet the whole room spinning as blood drips from your hair. The Dremora screams "For Lord Dagon" as he presses the attack! Death is calling you... You can either run away hoping to recuperate. However this is Oblivion, spikes line the walls and protrude from the columns. You have one final choice. Run and die or fight to your dying breath...
