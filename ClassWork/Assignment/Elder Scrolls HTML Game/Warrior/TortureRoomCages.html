The Deadlands: An Elder Scrolls Adventure

Torture Chamber

Despite the mutilation, blood, and gore you recognise the corpses of the men you came here with. Good men, and strong soldiers. They were Imperial Guards just like yourself before being ripped apart and brutally murdered. The sight makes you sick to your stomach but it also makes you angry. A red mist descends on you, hoping to rip apart the next Dremora you see...
